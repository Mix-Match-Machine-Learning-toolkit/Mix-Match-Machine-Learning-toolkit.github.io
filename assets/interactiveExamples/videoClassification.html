Video classification

This model categorizes videos in their entirety and predicts what is visible in the video. It outputs the top 5 classes together with their confidence scores, i.e. how certain the model is.

Models: HuggingFace Transformers video models
Data: Kinetics (and more)
Example from: Fatih on Huggingface
